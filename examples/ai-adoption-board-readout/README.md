# Synthetic AI Adoption Board Readout

This example shows how to turn AI adoption into an executive operating review instead of a tooling update.

All metrics are synthetic.

## Scenario

A data organization has completed a one-month pilot of AI-assisted analytics in one recurring decision forum. The CDO needs to brief leadership on progress, risk, and next decisions.

## Inputs

| Input | Example |
| --- | --- |
| Pilot domain | Growth weekly review |
| Pilot metrics | 10 decision-critical metrics |
| Verified metric packets | 7 of 10 |
| Recurring questions with approved source paths | 68% |
| Answer consistency across repeated phrasings | 86% |
| Reconciliation loops | down from 14 to 6 |
| Manager-reviewed outputs | 42 |

## Output

Use [`../../docs/sample-monthly-cdo-review.md`](../../docs/sample-monthly-cdo-review.md) as the finished board-ready artifact.

## What To Look For

- Business impact, trust, team leverage, and governance are shown together.
- The review asks for decisions, not applause.
- Expansion is gated on trust thresholds.
- Risks are explicit enough for leadership to act.
