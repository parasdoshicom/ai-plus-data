# Manager AI Adoption Scorecard

Use this scorecard to review whether your team's AI adoption is creating real leverage or just more noise.

Score each area from 1 to 5.

## 1. Workflow Repeatability

1 = everyone is improvising
3 = a few repeatable patterns exist
5 = the team has clear, shared workflows for the main use cases

## 2. Validation Discipline

1 = review is informal or skipped
3 = some important work is reviewed
5 = every meaningful AI-assisted output has a defined validation step

## 3. Data Safety

1 = unclear boundaries
3 = some safe practices exist
5 = approved environments, data rules, and red lines are documented and followed

## 4. Manager Coaching

1 = managers mainly ask whether people are using AI
3 = managers discuss tools and prompts
5 = managers actively coach workflow design, review standards, and use-case selection

## 5. Business Impact

1 = lots of experimentation, little measurable outcome
3 = a few visible wins
5 = clear gains in speed, throughput, quality, or stakeholder responsiveness

## 6. Knowledge Capture

1 = lessons are lost between sessions
3 = some examples are saved
5 = reusable prompts, corrections, examples, and patterns are captured in a shared system

## 7. Trust

1 = stakeholders do not trust AI-assisted outputs
3 = trust depends on who produced the work
5 = outputs are trusted because the workflow and validation are trusted

## Interpreting the Score

- 7-14: experimentation phase, not yet operational
- 15-24: promising, but inconsistent
- 25-30: reliable adoption foundation
- 31-35: strong operating model, ready to scale further

## Review Questions

Use these in a weekly or monthly manager review:

- Where did AI help this month in a way that mattered?
- Where did it create rework?
- Which workflow should we standardize next?
- What should remain human-led?
- What would increase trust most over the next month?
