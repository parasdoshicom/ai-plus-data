## Internal Source Handling

Private experience may inform the public writing, but private material is not copied directly into this repo. Any operator pattern drawn from experience must be:

1. meaningfully rewritten
2. generalized beyond one company
3. stripped of names, metrics, links, IDs, and confidential context
4. useful as public educational material on its own

## Exclusions

This repo intentionally excludes:

- private company strategy
- internal SQL, schemas, and warehouse identifiers
- customer, employee, candidate, or operational data
- private dashboards, doc IDs, URLs, or chat links
- compensation, performance, immigration, or HR-related material

See [`repo-privacy-policy.md`](./repo-privacy-policy.md) and [`publication-safety-checklist.md`](./publication-safety-checklist.md).
